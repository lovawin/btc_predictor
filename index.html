Built Different

Smart guards.
Real edge.

Every entry is run through multiple filters before a single dollar moves. Most bots trade noise. This one doesn't.

🧠

Confidence Filter

Only executes when the signal crosses your minimum confidence threshold. Set it at 65%, 70%, 75% — your call. Weak signals get skipped entirely.

📐

Edge Filter

Checks that net expected return clears the cost buffer before entry. Filters out "technically bullish but barely" setups that eat fees and lose.

⏱️

Resolution Timing

Tracks seconds remaining in each 5-minute slot. Skips new entries when it's too close to expiry — no more entering trades with 30 seconds left.

📉

Macro Trend Lock

Won't fight a strong 5-hour BTC trend. If the macro is moving hard one direction, the bot stops trying to call reversals. Trade with the tape.

🔒

Circuit Breaker

Automatic pause after hitting daily loss limits or consecutive loss streaks. Configurable multipliers and cooldown periods. Protects your capital on bad days.

🛡️

DCA Guards

Blocks averaging into a losing position when the token has already dropped too far from your cost basis. No spiraling deeper into bad trades.

Command Reference

Everything at
your fingertips.

Full control from Telegram. Every parameter is adjustable in real time — no restarts, no config files.

/start Begin your private session
/setkey Connect your wallet private key
/setmax 20 Set max bet size in USD
/setstops 8 4 Take profit % and stop loss %
/starttrade Start the auto trading loop
/stoptrade Pause all trading activity
/status Live signal, confidence & position
/pnl Your win/loss record and P&L
/myconfig All current settings in one view
/setminconf 65 Min confidence to enter a trade
/setminedge 1 Min net expected edge %
/setresolvebuf 90 Skip entries if slot ending soon
/settrendlock 1.5 Block trades fighting 5h trend
/setcb 2 3 30 Circuit breaker: mult / consec / cooldown
/cbstatus Live circuit breaker state
/runonce Run a single manual cycle
